About Episode One

What We Do

The Database

Episode One is a curated database of television pilot screenplays. We only post pilots that have aired, giving aspiring writers a chance to study the work that actually made it to screen.

Who We're For

The Audience

Whether you're an aspiring television writer, a student of the craft, or simply someone with a natural proclivity for reading teleplays — Episode One was built for you.

Why We Exist

The Mission

We believe great television starts with great writing. Our mission is to make the blueprints of great TV accessible to the next generation of storytellers.
